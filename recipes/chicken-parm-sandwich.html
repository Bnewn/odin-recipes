Chicken Parmesan Sandwich Recipe

The best way to make a chicken parmasan sandwich

Satisfy your cravings with the delectable delight of a Chicken Parmesan Sandwich merges comfort and flavor in every bite.

Picture succulent breaded chicken, cooked to golden perfection and layered with rich marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese. As you take your first bite, the crispy chicken gives way to tender, juicy goodness, complemented by the vibrant tang of the marinara and the creamy, melted cheese. This sandwich is a harmony of textures and tastes, combining the crunch of the breading with the gooey melt of cheese. Elevate your lunchtime experience or indulge in a hearty dinner with this classic favorite that's both comforting and utterly delicious. Treat your taste buds to a symphony of flavors that captures the essence of Italian-inspired comfort food.

Ingredients needed

  • Chicken breast
  • Bread
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt, pepper, garlic, paprika, basil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix bread crumbs, garlic powder, peprika, salt, pepper, and dried basil leaves, put into shallow bowl
  2. Whisk eggs and put into shallow bowl
  3. Put flour in third shallow bowl
  4. Pound chicken until about more than half an inch thick
  5. Dredge chicken by dipping cutlets in the following order; flour, egg, and breadcrumbs
  6. Heat oil until medium hot and fry the chicken, approximately 4 minutes per side
  7. Place chicken on a rack and top off with mozzerella cheese
  8. Cut burns in half, add cooked chicken cutlet, and top off with hot marinara sauce
