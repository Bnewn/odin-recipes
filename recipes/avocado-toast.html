Avocado Toast

The best way to make avocado toast

Elevate your breakfast game with the irresistible charm of avocado toast; a culinary masterpiece that's both nutritious and indulgent.

Imagine creamy avocado, perfectly ripe and adorned on toasted artisanal bread. With each bite, you'll experience a delightful fusion of velvety textures and fresh flavors. The creamy avocado mingles harmoniously with the toasty crunch of the bread, creating a symphony of taste that's both satisfying and wholesome. Whether you're looking for a quick morning bite or a stylish brunch option, avocado toast is here to enchant your taste buds with its simple yet exquisite allure. Dive into a delightful adventure of flavors and textures that's as versatile as it is delicious, finished with your toppings of choice!

Ingredients needed

  • Bread
  • Avocado
  • Garlic salt and pepper
  • Basil
  • Cilantro
  • Tomato
  • Optional: Egg

Instructions

  1. Toast slice of bread until golden and firm
  2. Slice avocado in half and remove the pit
  3. Scoop out avocado into a bowl
  4. Add inclusions, salt, pepper, garlic, etc
  5. Mash avocado until desired texture
  6. Spread avocado on top of the toast and sprinkle a little pepper or sea salt for taste
  7. Top off avocado toast with a sunny side up egg for extra protein or slices of tomato
